About Us

Alex Ravski is a venture capital and M&A attorney and the founder of Nebo Legal, P.C., a Silicon Valley boutique advising startups and investors on corporate and financing matters. He serves as fractional General Counsel to early-stage and growth companies, guiding venture financings, acquisitions, governance structuring, and complex founder dynamics.

His background includes experience at an Am Law 50 firm, as well as roles in compliance and investment banking. He has advised dozens of startups across legal and operational matters, and serves on the board of a 501(c)(3).

An entrepreneur at heart, Alex works closely with founders to build clean, scalable structures-aligning legal architecture with capital strategy, long-term ownership design, and investor readiness.
